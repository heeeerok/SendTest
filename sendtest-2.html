How it works

Three steps to a clean send

No account needed. No install. Works with any ESP — Klaviyo, Mailchimp, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, or any custom template.

STEP 01
Export your email HTML
In your ESP, find the option to view or export the rendered HTML of your campaign. In Klaviyo it's "Preview" → "View HTML". In Mailchimp it's "Preview" → "Edit HTML".
STEP 02
Paste and run
Paste the HTML into the box above. Optionally add your subject line and production domain. Click Run QA Check. Results appear in under a second.
STEP 03
Fix issues and send
Review the report. Fix any critical issues flagged in red. Re-run to confirm. When the verdict shows "Clear to send" — you're good to go.
What we check

Automatic checks vs. human review

SendTest catches structural errors automatically. Some errors require human judgment — we flag those too so nothing slips through.

✓ Caught automatically
✓
Duplicate URLs across different CTAs
Catches the exact アソビュー error — two listings copy-pasted to the same wrong URL
✓
Unresolved merge tags
Flags {{variable}}, *|MERGE_TAG|*, and ${var} left unreplaced in links or subject lines
✓
Staging / dev environment URLs
Detects staging., dev., qa., localhost and similar patterns
✓
Placeholder # links
Flags href="#" — not clickable in Outlook, Gmail, or Apple Mail

✓
Missing image alt text
Flags functional images without alt text — critical for accessibility and image-off rendering
